AI Powered Voice Assistance for Museum of London

Facilities managers face unexpected daily challenges, low availability of resources, and high-quality expectations. The cause of stress is lack of time followed by lack of budget resources to perform duties and fast changing demands of the job.

The Museum of London plans maintenance using the AMB Technology Industry Solutions service. Emergency information is communicated via WhatsApp groups. Shift handover is done using handwritten sheets.

The proposed solution is an AI enabled voice assistant enables the staff to listen to their daily tasks before and/or during their shift. The product replicates the ABM system with extra features to improve the shift handover and task management. The solution will provide a daily briefing for the staff accessing it.
